### What I Do

I build mods for **[Icarus: First Cohort](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1149460/Icarus/)** — a survival game by RocketWerkz. My mod collection covers everything from processing speed to hidden building pieces, turret systems, economy overhauls, and full difficulty rebalances.

All mods use the **EXMODZ** format for [JimK72's Mod Manager](https://github.com/Jimk72/Icarus_Software) and are 100% free and open source.
